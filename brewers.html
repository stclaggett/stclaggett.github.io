Project 2: the Website Redesign
This project was straight forward. Redesign the website of a well-known, well-liked Baltimore restaurant with a website leaving a bit to be desired. Keep in mind layout, anticipate the objectives of those visiting the site, and strive for a feel that captures the character of the dining establishment. I chose to do the Brewer's Art. In my final version of the design, I incorporated the sophisticated yet slightly grungy feel that I hadn't quite captured in my previous versions of the design.
